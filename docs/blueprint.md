# **App Name**: DeutschCV

## Core Features:

- Germany CV Guide & Best Practices: Displays comprehensive guide content covering German CV best practices, ATS rules, standard section order, and critical formatting details.
- Interactive CV Builder: Provides a section-by-section form for users to input their personal, experience, education, skills, and other details to construct their CV.
- Contextual Germany-Specific Guidance: Offers integrated warnings and tips throughout the CV builder forms to ensure adherence to German CV standards, ATS optimization, and common pitfalls.
- ATS-Optimized Content Refinement Tool: A generative AI tool that helps users refine and optimize their entered CV content (e.g., job descriptions, profile summaries) to make it ATS-friendly and suitable for the German job market, based on provided guidelines.
- Before/After CV Examples Showcase: Presents illustrative before-and-after CV examples demonstrating how to convert generic international content into ATS-optimized, Germany-ready formats.
- CV Quality Checklist & Review: An interactive one-page checklist for users to review their CV against key quality criteria and German recruitment expectations before finalization.
- Multi-Format CV Export: Allows users to download their generated, customized CV in ATS-friendly PDF and editable DOCX formats.

## Style Guidelines:

- A sophisticated dark charcoal with a warm undertone (#383430) serves as the primary color for professionalism and authority, contrasting cleanly with the light scheme.
- The background features a subtle, very light warm gray (#F3F1EF) for a clean, spacious feel, inspired by a muted gold and optimal readability.
- A professional, muted red (#CC3333) acts as the accent color for call-to-action buttons, key highlights, and important information, subtly referencing the 'red' from the German flag in a refined manner.
- Headlines use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) to convey a modern, slightly technical, and precise feel, reflecting ATS optimization concepts.
- Body text utilizes 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional readability, neutral character, and suitability for forms and extended guide content, ensuring clarity and professionalism.
- Use minimal, professional line-based icons that intuitively represent job search steps, document elements, and career achievements without cluttering the interface.
- A clean, well-structured layout emphasizing clear information hierarchy, generous whitespace, and mobile responsiveness across all devices, supporting a straightforward user journey.
- Subtle and purposeful animations are employed for form transitions, hover effects on interactive elements, and content reveals, enhancing user engagement without distraction.
